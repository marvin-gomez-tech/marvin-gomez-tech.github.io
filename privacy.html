Privacy Policy — TikTokClipperBot

Last updated: June 21, 2026

TikTokClipperBot ("the App") is a personal automation tool operated by its owner to repost the owner's own livestream clips to the owner's own TikTok account. This policy explains what data the App handles.

Who this applies to. The App is a single-user personal tool. It has no public end users, accounts, or sign-ups.

Data the App accesses.

  • TikTok access tokens obtained through TikTok's official OAuth, used solely to upload videos to the authorized TikTok account. Tokens are stored locally on the operator's own computer and are never shared.
  • Publicly available livestream clip metadata and video files from Twitch and Kick, used to produce short-form vertical videos.

How data is used. Solely to create and upload short videos to the operator's own authorized TikTok account via TikTok's Content Posting API.

Data sharing. The App does not sell, share, or transmit personal data to any third party. Videos are sent only to TikTok, at the operator's direction.

Data retention. Access tokens remain on the operator's local machine until revoked. Generated video files are deleted on a rolling schedule.

Your choices. Access can be revoked at any time from TikTok account settings, which immediately stops the App's access.
